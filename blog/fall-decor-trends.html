Published September 2025 | CozyHome Team

Every fall brings new decor inspiration. This year, it’s all about cozy minimalism and natural touches. Let’s dive into the hottest trends you’ll see everywhere this season.

1. Natural Textures

Think jute rugs, woven baskets, and wood accents. See our picks.

2. Warm Metallics

Brass candle holders and gold accents elevate a cozy vibe. Shop metallic decor.
